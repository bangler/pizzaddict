About PizzAddict

Fresh from PizzAddict

It was 1990. The location is Batac City, IN. Percival Riego, our creator, saw that his neighborhood needed mouthwatering pizzas served with genuine Batac City friendliness. PizzAddict was the solution; it's a friendly neighborhood hangout with some of the tastiest, most unique flavors in town. It soon became the gathering spot for loved ones to have delicious meals and create enduring memories. Even after more than two decades, PizzAddict remains one of Batac City's oldest and most enduring single-family businesses.

Pizza goodness that melts in your mouth!

At PizzAddict, our mission is to deliver the best products possible and to give our community and cherished guests unwaveringly compassionate service. We are working to establish PizzAddict locations all over the world because we believe that everyone should always have a fantastic pizza experience. We hope to see you soon at a nearby PizzAddict location!
